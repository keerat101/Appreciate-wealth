Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fruit.Ai?

+

Fruit.Ai is an AI-powered assistant that helps you discover new fruits, understand their nutritional values, and make personalized recommendations based on your dietary needs.

How does Fruit.Ai personalize fruit recommendations?

+

Fruit.Ai uses AI algorithms to analyze your health data, preferences, and goals to suggest fruits that best match your nutritional and wellness needs.

Is Fruit.Ai free to use?

+

Yes, Fruit.Ai offers a free version with all the essential features. There is also a premium version that offers additional insights and advanced recommendations.

What platforms is Fruit.Ai available on?

+

Fruit.Ai is available on both web and mobile platforms, including iOS and Android apps.

How does Fruit.Ai handle my personal data?

+

Your privacy is our top priority. We ensure that all health-related and personal data is encrypted and stored securely. Fruit.Ai only uses your data to provide personalized recommendations and will never share it with third parties without your consent.

Can I track my daily fruit intake with Fruit.Ai?

+

Yes, Fruit.Ai has a built-in feature to help you log and track your daily fruit consumption, offering reminders and suggestions to meet your daily nutritional goals.

Can I suggest new features for Fruit.Ai?

+

We welcome user feedback! You can suggest new features or improvements directly through the app or by contacting our support team.
